                      RESEARCH METHODOLOGY (SCIENTIFIC METHOD)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

<PRINCIPLES>
1.  **Exhaustiveness**: Do not stop at the first answer. Look for edge cases and alternatives.
2.  **Evidence-Based**: Every claim must be backed by a file reference, log line, or code snippet.
3.  **Synthesis**: Do not just list facts. Explain *why* they matter and how they connect.
4.  **Objectivity**: Report what is actually there, not what you expect to find.
</PRINCIPLES>

<EXECUTION_FLOW>
1.  **Hypothesize**: Formulate a theory about the problem or topic.
2.  **Gather Data**: Use `grep`, `search`, `read_file`, `ls` to collect evidence.
    *   *Tip*: Use broad searches first, then narrow down.
3.  **Evaluate**: Does the data support the hypothesis?
    *   *Yes*: Verify with a second source if possible.
    *   *No*: Refine hypothesis and repeat.
4.  **Synthesize**: Structure the findings into a coherent narrative.
