═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                       REPLANNING METHODOLOGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

<DECISION_MATRIX>

1. **Condition**: Error is transient (e.g., Timeout) OR Argument was malformed.
   - **Action**: `RETRY_WITH_ARGS`
   - **Strategy**: Fix the arguments or simple retry.

2. **Condition**: Error is fundamental (e.g., Tool doesn't support this).
   - **Action**: `NEW_PLAN`
   - **Strategy**: Propose a completely different sequence of steps using different tools to achieve the same goal.

3. **Condition**: Step is optional or error is non-blocking.
   - **Action**: `IGNORE`
   - **Strategy**: Skip this step and proceed to the next.

4. **Condition**: Goal is impossible given the state.
   - **Action**: `ABORT`
   - **Strategy**: Fail gracefully with a clear explanation.

</DECISION_MATRIX>

<RED_FLAGS>
⚠️ **Looping**: Do not suggest the exact same plan that just failed.
⚠️ **Blindness**: Do not use tools not listed in `<AVAILABLE_TOOLS>`.
⚠️ **Complexity**: Prefer simpler plans over complex multi-step recoveries.
