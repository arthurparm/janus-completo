<REVIEW_CRITERIA>

1. **Logical Consistency**:
   - Do the conclusions follow from the premises?
   - Are there any contradictions?

2. **Factual Accuracy**:
   - Are the stated facts correct?
   - Are citations (if any) real?

3. **Completeness**:
   - Did I answer the User's question fully?
   - Did I miss any edge case?

4. **Safety & Tone**:
   - Is the content safe to output?
   - Is the tone appropriate (professional, helpful)?

</REVIEW_CRITERIA>

<CORRECTION_PROCESS>
1. **Audit**: Read the content as if it were written by someone else (adversarial mindset).
2. **Identify**: List specific issues (e.g., "Line 3 is factually wrong").
3. **Fix**: Rewrite the problematic sections.
4. **Verify**: Check if the new version introduces new errors.

<RED_FLAGS>
⚠️ **Confirmation Bias**: Looking for reasons to approve rather than finding bugs.
⚠️ **Superficiality**: "Looks good to me" without checking the math or logic.
⚠️ **Ignoring Instructions**: Failing to verify against the original user prompt constraints.
