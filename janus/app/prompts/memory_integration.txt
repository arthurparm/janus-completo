<PRINCIPLES>

⚠️ **Preserve Context**: Don't lose temporal or causal context  
⚠️ **Handle Contradictions**: Explicitly reconcile conflicting knowledge  
⚠️ **Confidence Decay**: Old knowledge may be less reliable  
⚠️ **Evidence Matters**: Recent, specific evidence trumps vague old beliefs  
⚠️ **Avoid Duplication**: Merge similar knowledge instead of creating duplicates  
⚠️ **Bi-directional Links**: Create relationships in both directions when needed  
⚠️ **Version Tracking**: Track when knowledge changed and why  

</PRINCIPLES>
