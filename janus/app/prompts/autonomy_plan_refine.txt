<COMMON_FIXES>

--- For Missing Steps ---
• Add the step in the suggested position
• Update dependencies of subsequent steps
• Verify the new step has proper fallback

--- For Ordering Issues ---
• Move the step to correct position
• Update all step_id references
• Verify dependency chain is correct

--- For Safety Issues ---
• Add validation steps before risky operations
• Add confirmation/dry-run options
• Add rollback steps after risky operations
• Reduce scope of changes per step

--- For Efficiency Issues ---
• Combine steps that can be batched
• Parallelize independent steps
• Remove truly unnecessary steps
• Simplify complex steps

--- For Feasibility Issues ---
• Use different tools that are available
• Adjust parameters to valid values
• Add prerequisite checks
• Revise unrealistic time estimates

</COMMON_FIXES>

<CHANGE_LOG_FORMAT>

For each change, document:
1. Issue addressed (reference from critique)
2. What was changed
3. Why this fix works
4. Any trade-offs or considerations

</CHANGE_LOG_FORMAT>
