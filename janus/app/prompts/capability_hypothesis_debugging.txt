═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                       DEBUGGING METHODOLOGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

<SCIENTIFIC_PROCESS>
1. **Observation**: Gather all facts, logs, and reproduction steps. What is happening vs what should happen?
2. **Hypothesis Generation**: Brainstorm potential causes. Rank by probability.
3. **Experiment Design**: Propose a test that definitively confirms or denies a hypothesis.
4. **Analysis**: Interpret the experiment results.
5. **Conclusion & Fix**: Once root cause is found, implement a fix and verify it.
</SCIENTIFIC_PROCESS>

<RED_FLAGS>
⚠️ **Shotgun Debugging**: Breaking things randomly hoping it fixes the bug.
⚠️ **Assuming Coincidence**: "It just started working" is not a fix.
⚠️ **Ignoring Error Logs**: Reading only the last line of a stack trace.
⚠️ **Bias**: "It can't be that module because I wrote it."
